Daily Meal Timing

Your meals placed at optimal eating times for the day. Set your wake, gym (2-hour session), and sleep times — or check No gym today — and the schedule recalculates: breakfast shortly after waking, a light pre-gym snack, a protein-forward dinner after lifting, and dessert with a buffer before bed.

Shared Ingredients

Pick meals that reuse the same ingredients so one week's shopping and prep overlap. Recipes are ranked by how many ingredient names they share. Choose a recipe under Compare against to see what overlaps with a favorite.

Week Templates

Create, edit, or delete preset meal combinations (Breakfast / Meal 1 / Meal 2 / Dessert). They appear in the Load Template picker on the Dashboard. Sign in to make changes.
